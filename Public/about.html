Hi, I'm Simon Luckhoff

By day I am an avid car photographer and by night I'm a wannabe coder.

My passion lays with cars ever since I was a little boy. Later on in High School I was exposed to taking photos and my love for photography began. Merging the two seemed like the right thing to do.

From 2021 to 2023 I completed my Bachelors degree in Visual Communication, majoring in Art Direction. Ever since I can remember, I took on freelance jobs. In 2021 I began work for The Archive Automotive.

My life completely changed, I went from a little boy taking photos of his dog to taking photos of some of the most rare cars in South Africa.

Recently I began this coding journey (yes, I coded this website), something I was exposed to very briefly during my degree.
